Quinoa Salad with Roasted Vegetables

Description

This Quinoa Salad with Roasted Vegetables is not only flavorful but also packed with nutrients. It's a great option for a light lunch, dinner, or even as a side dish for a barbecue or picnic. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups water or vegetable broth
  • 1 medium-sized zucchini, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • Feta cheese, crumbled (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a saucepan, combine quinoa and water or vegetable broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat, cover, and simmer for about 15 minutes or until quinoa is cooked and water is absorbed. Fluff the quinoa with a fork and set aside to cool.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, combine the diced zucchini, red and yellow bell peppers, cherry tomatoes, and sliced red onion.
  4. In a small bowl, mix minced garlic with olive oil, salt, pepper, and dried oregano. Drizzle this mixture over the vegetables and toss until well coated.
  5. Spread the vegetables on a baking sheet in a single layer. Roast in the preheated oven for about 20-25 minutes or until the vegetables are tender and slightly browned.
  6. In a large serving bowl, combine the cooked quinoa with the roasted vegetables.
  7. Squeeze fresh lemon juice over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  8. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley and crumbled feta cheese (if using).
  9. Adjust salt and pepper to taste.
  10. Serve the quinoa salad warm or chilled.
